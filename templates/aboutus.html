    Our purpose for creating “Meet-a-Mate” is to help students above 9th grade that are new or transfered to highschool to find someone that can guide them through the school year.


    Our team developed this website from the fact that we were new students in highschool. We both realized that we had a hard time adjusting because there wasn't anyone to guide us. We wanted to create a community where new students can be matched with mentors that could help guide them to a fun highschool experience!
